Cyberpunk 2077's Latest Patch
Introduces Playable Cybernetic Pets

Night City, known for its neon-soaked streets and cyber-enhanced denizens, is about to get a lot furrier. In a surprising move that has thrilled players, CD Projekt Red has rolled out Patch 1.5 for Cyberpunk 2077, introducing an innovative feature: playable cybernetic pets.

Gone are the days of roaming the bustling metropolis alone; now, players can enhance their experience by adopting their very own cybernetic companions. Choose from a variety of cyber-animals—mechanical felines, robotic canines, or even augmented avian drones. These pets aren't just cosmetic; they bring a whole new dimension to gameplay.

Players can customize their cyber-pets with a range of cyberware upgrades, from enhanced agility modules to stealth capabilities, turning them into indispensable allies in the treacherous Night City. Picture a cyber-hound hacking security systems or a sleek robotic cat distracting enemies with its laser pointer while you infiltrate a megacorp's headquarters.

The functionality doesn't stop there. These cybernetic companions are fully integrated into Night City's social fabric. Interact with other NPCs and their cyber-pets, engage in cybernetic pet battles in underground arenas, or embark on side quests tailored specifically for these mechanical companions.

To ensure players truly connect with their cyber-friends, an emotional bonding system has been implemented. Spend time with your cyber-pet, go on walks through Night City's dark alleys, and witness unique interactions based on your actions and choices. Feel the loyalty grow as you traverse the sprawling urban landscape together.

However, with great companionship comes great responsibility. Maintenance and care are crucial; cyber-pets need regular cybernetic check-ups, upgrades, and occasional recharging to maintain their optimal functionality. Ignoring their needs might lead to unpredictable behavior or, worse, a malfunction during a crucial mission.

Patch 1.5 has breathed new life into Cyberpunk 2077, fostering a deeper connection between players and Night City's tech-infused world. As players traverse the cityscape, now they have a metallic companion by their side—a loyal, cybernetic friend ready to pounce into action and hack their way through the cybernetic wilderness of Night City.
